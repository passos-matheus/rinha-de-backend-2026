# FAQ — frequently asked questions

## What's allowed and what isn't

**Allowed:**
- Use any language / framework / database / technology
- Use vector databases (pgvector, Qdrant, SQLite-vss, etc.) inside your compose
- Pre-process the dataset during container build
- Use exact KNN, approximate ANN, or any other classifier
- Get hyperfocused because of Rinha
- Suggest improvements, report issues, etc. for this Rinha edition (any contribution is very welcome – just open a pull request). I'm a one-person show and I've certainly made mistakes.
- Just do the challenge at home, without showing anyone, without submitting, because that's what you want to do and it makes you happy.
- Date during Rinha.
- Date while your backend is in the queue to be tested.
- Have a kid or two during Rinha.
- Start exercising so your mind gets fresher and you think of a better solution for your backend.
- Move to the countryside during Rinha.
- Move to the city during Rinha.
- Learn a new language or technique to use in Rinha.
- Vibe-code and burn all your tokens to build your backend.
- Use pen and paper to write your Rinha code.

**Not allowed:**
- Use a load balancer that does any fraud-detection-related logic
- Try to "hide" your submission so others don't learn from it
- Demand anything (other than respect) from those who organize or participate in Rinha. Nobody is making money from this and we're here to have fun and learn.
- Disrespect anyone participating in Rinha
- Skip work because of Rinha
- Forget to drink water because of Rinha
- Fight with the dog because of Rinha
- Curse out relatives because of Rinha
- Wear socks with flip-flops because of Rinha
- Use alcohol excessively just because your p99 is too high

## Common mistakes
- Not making your docker image from docker-compose.yml public
- For Mac users, creating docker images that are only arm64-compatible
- Not making your git repository public
